Cloudiness

Analysis: As one moves from pole to equator the air temperature warms significantly. This warmer air traps particulate matter and water, facilitating cloud formation. That these tracks in bands along the latitudinal bands is likely just an artifact of that's where the geo-stational satellites are situated, so there's more data records that, not necessarily zebra stripes of clouds circling the earth.
